# AGENTS.md Policy

## Purpose

This document defines how `AGENTS.md` should be written and maintained.

## Core Rules

- Treat `AGENTS.md` as an indexing and routing document whenever possible.
- Keep always-relevant guidance in `AGENTS.md`.
- Move occasional or specialized rules into companion reference documents.
- Avoid turning `AGENTS.md` into a monolithic catch-all rules file.

## What Belongs In AGENTS.md

- communication rules that should affect nearly every turn
- delegation and routing rules
- high-level interaction defaults
- short links to companion reference documents

## What Should Usually Be Moved Out

- detailed workflow procedures
- release or versioning steps
- long policy explanations
- niche operational conventions that are not needed on most turns

## Writing Style

- keep sections short and scannable
- prefer compact rules over long essays
- use references when detail is needed
